The Laboratory

Designing with
people and purpose.

THE O ARCHITECTURE LAB is an independent architecture and research laboratory founded in Istanbul. We operate at the intersection of design, sustainability, building biology and social transformation — carrying projects from conceptual research to physical realisation.

Educated at ITU and TU Munich, our founding director brings 9 years of built and unbuilt work spanning residential, civic, competition and humanitarian fields. The lab collaborates with universities, development agencies and private clients across 14 countries.

Our Process

How we
think and build.

Every project begins with listening. We invest heavily in the discovery phase — understanding context, people and ambition before a single line is drawn. From there, design unfolds as a continuous dialogue between idea and reality.

Begin a Project →
I

Listen & Discover

Deep briefing, site research, cultural and contextual analysis. We ask the questions others skip — because the right questions yield the right architecture.

II

Design & Develop

From loose sketches to precise drawings. An iterative, collaborative process where client, consultant and context shape every decision. No black-box design.

III

Build & Steward

Construction documentation, on-site presence and post-occupancy evaluation. We don't disappear once the drawings are issued — we stay with the work until it lives.

IV

Reflect & Publish

Every project returns to the research cycle. We document, analyse and share findings — building collective knowledge for the discipline.
